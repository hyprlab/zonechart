Refresh re-downloads the official chart for every origin prefix from ups.com through the app’s built-in browser engine, pacing requests a couple of seconds apart — a full forced run takes about 45 minutes. Without “force” it only fetches charts that are missing or invalid. New charts go live in the app the moment the run finishes. UPS typically updates rates in late December.
